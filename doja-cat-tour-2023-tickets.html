    How can I buy tickets to Doja Cat's 2023 Scarlet tour?

    In order to see Doja Cat on her Scarlet Tour, listeners can purchase tickets when the sales go live via Ticketmaster, as well as her official website.

    In an effort to filter out the fans from the bots and resale poachers, Ticketmaster is holding a presale registration.

    Registration starts on June 23, 2023, and concludes on Sunday, June 25, at 10pm PST.

    When the registration closes, fans who signed up will then be selected at random to receive a specified code.

    Read More on Doja Cat

    The specified code gives the Doja enthusiast access to the presale that goes live on Wednesday, June 28.

    Two days later, the Scarlet Tour's general sale begins on Friday, June 30, at 10am local time.

    VIP packages are also available at VIP Nation and the offer consists of multiple experiences fans can take advantage of.

    The VIP option includes premium tickets, exclusive photo opportunities, access to the pre-show VIP Lounge, and specially designed VIP gift items.

    Who is Doja Cat bringing on tour?

    For The Scarlet Tour, Doja is supported by Munch hitmaker Ice Spice and Florida-based rapper Doechii.

    Before the GRAMMY Award winner makes her entrance, the two rappers will open the show for her.

    This concert series is Doja's second headlining tour; her first was the 2018 to 2019 Amala tour in support of her debut album of the same name.

    After she released her 2021 album Planet Her, she was scheduled to support The Weeknd on his After Hours Til Dawn tour.

    However, Doja had to cancel because of her recovery from tonsil surgery.

    What are the tour dates?

    The Scarlet Tour kicks off on Halloween and finishes in the middle of December.
