    A BIG-BUSTED woman tested two corset tops to see if they were big boob-approved.

    The excited shopper liked both Asos options, but one needed to be adjusted as it was a little too peek-a-boo.

    Miranda Kaufman (@bigb00bapproved) took to her TikTok to share her shopping advice.

    She posted a video review of two corsets from Asos and liked both of them for the most part.

    The fashion fan asked: "Is Asos's fuller bust collection big boob-approved?"

    First, Miranda put on the satin option she bought. She held up the neon pink $27 Fuller Bust satin padded underwire corset.

    Unfortunately, her usual size 30F was unavailable. So, Miranda settled for the next best: 28H.

    This pink pick had detachable straps that didn't noticeably dig into Miranda's skin.

    Though the corset felt a tad tight around her midsection, Miranda was a fan of the look.

    Her boobs were perfectly propped, covered, and supported without any spillage on top or the side.

    Most read in Fashion

    "Guys, are you kidding me? I'm in love with this top. This looks so good," Miranda exclaimed.

    Before trying the next top, Miranda noticed a difference in how it looked online compared to in person.

    She grabbed the $40 Fuller Bust lace plunge underwire corset top in a 30H.

    However, the lace was way more see-through than she expected.

    "In the pictures on the website, this did not look see-through. It had a white lining behind it," Miranda proclaimed.

    The innovative woman had the idea to put nipple covers under the top.

    "This top is also amazing, fits super well," Miranda said.

    She warned viewers that it ran small, so they should size up as she did.

    Miranda added: "I ended up wearing the pink top for my bday and it was so comfortable all night."
