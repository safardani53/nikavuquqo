    Kyle Shanahan | Early Life and Education

    Houston Texans

    With the Houston Texans, Kyle Shanahan got his foremost job as a position coach. Gary Kubiak hired him as a wide receivers coach in 2006, and Kyle became the youngest position coach back then in NFL.

    San Francisco 49ers

    After two years career with the Falcons, the San Francisco 49ers hired Kyle as their head coach on February 6, 2017. Right after he joined the team, he bought success in their first preseason game, followed by their first regular-season game. 

    Hence, Kyle ended his first season with the team on five winning streaks but with six wins out of seven games they played.

    In 2018, he led the team for only four wins as their quarterback Garoppolo struggled with immense injury. 

    Likewise, the following 2019 season was a great victory for both Kyle and the team as they had eight games win. In addition, this season, the team bagged the NFC West division title and became the #1 seed. 

    Kyle Shanahan extended his contract with San Francisco on June 15, 2020, and later finished his season with a 6–10 record.
