    BESTIVAL'S little sister, Camp Bestival, is in full swing.

    The family-friendly event offers comedy shows, and workshops as well as musical performances. Here's when it's on and how you can get tickets...

    When is Camp Bestival and where is it held?

    Camp Bestival is on this weekend - from Thursday, July 27 to Sunday, July 30.

    The festival, which is a smaller, more family-oriented version of Bestival, has won awards for being the Best Family Festival in 2009, 2010, 2013 and 2014.

    It's held in the grounds of Lulworth Castle, on the Dorset coast, and is based around family-friendly entertainment alongside performances from big-name music acts.

    However, you'll find acts and entertainment for all ages at the festival, which is timed to coincide with the start of the school holidays.

    Who is playing at Camp Bestival?

    This year's line-up features a crop of typically family-friendly headline acts, including Madness, Mark Ronson and All Saints.

    Among the big names performing are Holly Johnson, Brian Wilson, Reverend and the Makers, Right Said Fred and Louis Berry.

    The live line-up is accompanied by an array of up-and-coming comedians, theatre shows, poets and authors.

    For the full line-up, check out the Camp Bestival website.

    Where can I get Camp Bestival tickets and how much do they cost?

    Tickets for this weekend are on sale through Ticketline.

    Adult weekend tickets and Friday and Saturday day tickets are all sold-out, but day tickets for Sunday and teenagers' weekend tickets are still available.

    A weekend ticket for a 13-17 year old will set teens back £134.35 including the booking fee.

    Meanwhile, day tickets for Sunday cost £71 for adults, whilst kids' tickets range from £7.75 to £46.20 depending on their age.

    What is the weather forecast for Camp Bestival?

    Unfortunately, the Met Office forecast a rainy Bestival weekend.

    Despite rainy weather on Friday and Saturday, damp campers may have a chance to dry out on Sunday, however, when the heavy rain is expected to give way.

    Instead, light showers are expected from 1pm through to the early evening.
