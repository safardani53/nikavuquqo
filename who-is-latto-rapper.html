    Who is Latto?

    Latto was born on December 22, 1998, in Columbus, Ohio. However, she moved to Atlanta, Georgia, in her teens.

    Latto, real name, Alyssa Michelle Stephens, is a rapper and songwriter who began her professional career in 2016.

    She first gained media attention after appearing on Jermaine Dupri's reality television series, The Rap Game, in 2016, where she starred as Miss Mulatto.

    She won the show's first season.

    Latto, who is also known as Big Latto, signed with RCA Records after releasing her 2019 single, Bitch from da Souf. She made a remix of her song with veteran rappers Trina and Saweetie.

    Furthermore, she released another single, Muwop, featuring Gucci Mane, in 2020.

    She released her album, Queen of da Souf, in August 2020.

    Despite her rising success, her mainstream breakthrough was after she released Big Energy, which is the lead single from her second studio album, 777, in 2022.

    As a result of her stellar performances, she has won one Georgia Music Award, one BET Award, and one BET Hip Hop Award.

    As an entrepreneur, she opened her own store, Pittstop Clothing, in Jonesboro, Georgia.

    What is Latto's net worth?

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Latto has an estimated net worth of approximately $2million, as of October 2022.

    Her main source of wealth is said to be the earnings from her music career.

    She also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.

    As of the time of writing, Latto is dating. However, she has decided to keep the name of her boyfriend private.

    However, there have been speculations that she is dating 21 Savage.
