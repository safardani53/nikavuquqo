    Christy Mack Thanks Her Supporters After Attack

    “I would like to thank everyone for the support and kind thoughts over the past few days. I will never be able to express my gratitude,” Christy tweeted.

    After Christy was attacked, she was left with an extensive list of injuries, including 18 broken bones in her face, a fractured rib, and a ruptured liver. She claims her ex-boyfriend, MMA fighter Jon Koppenhaver (aka War Machine), 32, is at fault. He, however, has denied viciously attacking Christy, whom he allegedly planned to propose to, in a series of tweets posted on Aug. 10.

    Regardless of who’s at fault, it’s going to take a while for Christy to recover from this brutal attack. Thankfully, her colleagues have started a fundraiser to raise money for her reconstructive surgeries, TMZ reports.

    Christy Mack: Porn Community Raising Money For Facial Reconstruction

    Kendall Karson, who is also an adult film star, launched a crowd-funding campaign.  So far, she has raised over $40K of the $100K goal.

    In addition to Kendall, Kendra Lust, Ava Addams and Tasha Reign have donated money. Dave Navarro and Jenna Jameson have also given their support.

    Thank you @KendallKarsonXo for your help and starting a fundraiser for my recovery, I know this is close to home. https://t.co/YQDufEL43Y

    — Christy Mack (@ChristyMack) August 13, 2014

    Christy has also thanked Dr. Toscano, who has offered to fix her teeth — and yes, he’s doing it for free, his rep confirmed to HollywoodLife.com.

    I would also like to thank @DrToscanoDDS for offering his services in fixing my teeth. I appreciate your support for all victims of abuse.

    — Christy Mack (@ChristyMack) August 13, 2014

    It’s nice to hear that Christy has so many people in her corner. She’s certainly going to need it as she recovers.
