Edwards then appeared in the second season of RuPaul's Drag Race All Stars and starred in his own web series, Alyssa's Secret. In June 2019, a New York magazine jury ranked her fifth on the list of "America's strongest drag queens," the ranking of RuPaul's Drag Race 100 alumni. Johnson lives in Mesquite, Texas, where he owns and operates a dance studio, Beyond Belief Dance Company. Johnson and his dance studio are the hub for the documentary store, Dancing Queen, produced by RuPaul and World of Wonder, which premiered October 5, 2018 on Netflix.
