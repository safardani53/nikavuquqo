    Aaron Carter was a singer who burst onto the music scene in the late 1990s and would have been 35 on December 7. He sadly passed away on November 5, 2022.

    Aaron Carter’s self-titled debut album was released in 1997 and it was followed up by his album, ‘Aaron’s Party (Come Get It)’ in 2000. went on to relese several other albums over the years, with his last being released in 2018. In addition to music, Aaron participated in a lot of other projects throughout his lifetime, including a stint on ‘Dancing With the Stars.’ He also appeared in the Broadway musical ‘Seussical’ and the off-Broadway musical ‘The Fantasticks.’

    Aaron was the younger brother of Nick Carter, who gained fame as a singer in the musical group, Backstreet Boys. He was also the brother of singer Leslie Carter, who sadly passed away at the age of 25 after an overdose of medications. Aaron and his family appeared on the E! reality show, ‘House of Carters’ in 2006. In 2021, Aaron welcomed a son Prince. Aaron sadly died by drowning at the age of 34 on Nov. 5, 2022. He had suffered from substance abuse issues for several years and had been to rehab.
