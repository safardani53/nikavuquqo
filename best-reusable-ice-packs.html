    What do ice packs do?

    When it comes to easing pain, an ice pack or cold pack is often the first thing we think about to do the trick. And for good reason.

    Icing an injury reduces blood flow to the area, which decreases inflammation and can even speed up recovery time. Ice also numbs the affected area, which helps with pain relief.

    Whether you've turned over your ankle playing badminton, your toddler has hit their head on the coffee table, or you suffer from painful arthritis inflammation – the right reusable ice pack can provide welcome relief.

    And if you have a raging fever, an ice pack on your forehead will also help to reduce your temperature.
