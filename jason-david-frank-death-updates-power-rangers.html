    Jason, known for his role as the Green ranger and later the White ranger in the popular television series Power Rangers, was in the middle of a divorce with Tammie when the pair reportedly checked into separate rooms at a Texas hotel on Friday night sources told TMZ.

    The outlet reported that the couple got into multiple arguments throughout the night causing hotel staffers to get involved to calm it down.

    Tammie then reportedly became concerned for his safety and called the cops around 5am on Saturday morning.

    According to law enforcement, when officers couldn't make contact with Jason, hotel management let them in and they found the actor's body in the bathroom.

    Jason is survived by his four children - Hunter, Jacob, Skye, and Jenna.

    His cause of death is reportedly believed to be by suicide, sources told TMZ.
