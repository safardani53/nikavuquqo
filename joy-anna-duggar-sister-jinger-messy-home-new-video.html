    19 Kids and Counting alum Joy-Anna Duggar has given fans a glimpse of her sister Jinger's messy home in Los Angeles.

    On Monday, Joy-Anna uploaded a new vlog on YouTube from her recent trip to California with her husband Austin Forsyth, and their two kids Gideon and Evelyn.

    The video, which was almost 10 minutes long, began with the family of four getting ready to head to the airport.

    "We are going to LA to visit Jinger and Jeremy for almost a week," Joy-Anna explained as Austin filmed her.

    "It's going to be so much fun. We're going to fly a direct flight which is gonna be amazing, so super excited!" she added.

    It then cut to them driving to the airport as Joy-Anna revealed they had managed to pack light for their vacation.

    She gave fans a glimpse of Evelyn sleeping on the plane and Gideon watching a TV show before they landed in sunny Los Angeles.

    Cutting to the next morning, Joy-Anna filmed her daughter Evelyn and Jinger's daughter Felicity singing "Happy Birthday."

    She revealed: "It's been such a nice day already, had breakfast and now we just ate lunch.

    "The kids are playing outside. They've not been able to play outside for a long time because our weather back home is freezing, but here it's beautiful weather," Joy-Anna added.

    The group then headed to a big playground and Joy-Anna filmed herself and her sister chilling on a bench with coffee as the kids played together.

    Jinger complained it was "a bit chilly" while Joy-Anna basked in the sunshine.

    Once back home, the families dug into a feast for dinner including spaghetti and salad.

    As Joy-Anna filmed her sibling serving up dinner, she panned the camera across the kitchen and the living room and exposed the mess.

    As Austin sat by the sofa and read a book with Gideon, toys, clothes, and shoes were strewn across the floor and the leather couch.

    The video then cut to Jo-Anna sitting outside as she ate dinner and revealed there were more toys cluttering up the backyard.

    As she filmed the sky as the sun set, she described her sister's $830,000 home as being like a "five-star resort."

    The vacation was the first time Jinger and Joy-Anna had reunited in a year after their suspected feud.

    Joy-Anna shared a sweet new video with the Counting On alum on her Instagram Stories last Wednesday.

    The video captured Jinger, 29, her husband Jeremy, 35, and Austin, 29, as the group played a game of dominoes.

    Joy-Anna captioned the video post: "Visited my sister @jingervuolo this past week," punctuated with a "red heart" emoji.

    During the recording, Jinger is shown wearing a gray winter sweater and giving her sister a funny face as the pregnant 26-year-old pans the camera around the room.

    Jeremy is captured with a mouth full of food as he attempts to explain the game while Austin listens to the directions from behind his smartphone.

    Joy-Anna also shared a photo of her daughter Evelyn, four, playing with her cousins Felicity, also four, and Evangeline, two.

    She wrote: "Evelyn LOVED playing with her cousins and all their princess dresses."

    The reality TV star also included a photo of herself and Jinger together on the Instagram Story.

    Joy-Anna held her pregnant belly as she donned a black-on-black outfit with a dark green jacket.

    The Becoming Free Indeed author wore a different gray sweater, matching it with a pair of loose-fitting denim jeans.
