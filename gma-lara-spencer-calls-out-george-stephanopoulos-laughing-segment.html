    LARA Spencer has showed off a whole new way to carve a pumpkin earlier this week on Good Morning America, however, it led to an awkward moment.

    She called out co-host George Stephanopoulos and Michael Strahan for laughing at her technique during a chaotic segment.

    On Thursday, the GMA panel discussed a viral TikTok video from "Grandma Babs" with special hacks to carve a pumpkin.

    According to Babs: "It is a treasured tradition in our family to carve jack-o-lanterns for Halloween.

    "But with all the yuck and mess, sometimes my kids would quit the project before we even started.

    "Babs’ Do’s and Don’ts for Pumpkin Carving, saves the mess and speeds up carving with no mess and no fuss."

    READ MORE GMA

    Among the elderly ladies' special tips are using special pumpkin carving cookie cutters and a rubber mallet to carve your design, covering all cut surfaces with Vaseline to keep the pumpkin moist after carving, and sprinkling some cinnamon on the inside top of the pumpkin for a festive pumpkin spice smell when a candle is lit inside the pumpkin.

    HILARIOUS HACK

    The hack that set off the male co-hosts was a suggestion to grab the pumpkin by the stem and hold it in your lap for a steadier cut.

    But Lara, 53, didn't have the space to sit with a pumpkin, and instead, lowered herself into an awkward squat.

    "Pretend I’m sitting and I’m using the stem and I’m really getting in there and I’m cutting the bottom open," Lara said, clutching the stem of the gourd.

    Most read in Entertainment

    The sight caused Michael and George to burst out in laughter, as Lara stared them down.

    "George, what is so funny?" she snapped.

    George shook his head and muttered, “I’m not going to go there.”

    Lara tried to gather herself and continue with another of Babs' tips about scooping out the innards, but found herself again distracted by George.

    "What she suggests we do is use this mixer, which I will aim at you George," she said. "And instead, this will loosen up all that gooey stuff."

    When it was all over, Michael said what everyone else was thinking: "I’ll be honest with you, George and I are looking at each other like what did we just watch?"

    CELEBRATING GEORGE

    George has plenty to smile about, after he and Robin Roberts were named the longest-serving pair of hosts ever on any ABC, CBS, and NBC morning show.

    The pair were named the hosts of GMA in 2009 and continue to appear on air 13 years later.

    George told the Associated Press that he’s proud that they’ve reached the major milestone.

    He said: “It’s been a great run. Robin has been a joy to work with.

    "We’ve had a lot of fun and great success and covered a lot of consequential things. It has been an incredible experience.”
