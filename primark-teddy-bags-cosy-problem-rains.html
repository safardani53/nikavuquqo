    A FASHIONISTA has revealed she’s discovered the perfect bags for the winter months, but trolls say there’s one issue with them. 

    Sophie Clare is a ‘midsize gal’ and often uses her social media channels to share her top finds, hacks and tips. 

    In one video, she revealed she did a haul at her local Primark, where she found an incredible array of teddy bags for the colder months. 

    “Primark bag haul because I just kept picking them up, I couldn’t help myself,” she said. 

    After showing off a crossbody bag, she moved straight onto a small square black handbag that had a furry exterior and a flap. 

    “I love the shape of this bag and the texture,” she told viewers.

    Revealing how she would style it, she dressed up into a silk midi skirt with a black oversized jumper.

    She then picked up the handbag and said: “Daywear for work and then straight out for drinks,” Sophie said. 

    The blonde beauty then changed into a grey jumper in a bid to “break it up a bit” but realised that outfit could work with an off-grey coloured bag, too. 

    TikTok user @sophieclarewhite then changed for a “little cosy girl moment” as she donned a pair of teal jogging bottoms with a matching long-sleeved top.

    Holding up a white printed teddy bear tote bag, she said: “First off all the print of the bag - love it, yes of course.”

    She then unzipped the top to show off the compartments inside, explaining that tote bags rarely have storage compartments on the inside. 

    “If you’re a student and you need to put some folders in or you’re going to a friends house for a cosy night in, take all your stuff in here - all your snacks - perfect,” she said.

    In the caption, she added: “Primark new in - bags! See how I style these four autumn bags (aka have an excuse to keep them all!)”. 

    People were quick to comment as one person wrote: “I went into primark, bought 3 bags I didn’t need,” followed by a crying with laughter emoji.

    Another said: “The excitement of ‘more zips’ is like finding a dress with pockets! Love it!” 

    But a third pointed out a major flaw in the bags as they said: “I love all the bags but I'm always wary of buying bags in that fluffy material kind of style. 

    “what if it rains and they get absolutely sodden,” followed by a crying with laughter emoji. 

    Sophie replied: “True, I hadn’t thought of that tbf!” but swiftly came up with a solution as she  added: “Big enough to pop a umbrella in!”
