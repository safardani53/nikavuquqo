    Joy-Anna Duggar Debuts New Fall Hairstyle!

    15 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Joy-Anna Duggar must be feelin’ festive because she just got the cutest new hairdo for the holidays and fans are in love. 

    On Wednesday, the Counting On star and her husband, Austin Forsyth, shared a photo to Instagram to pay a sweet tribute to their relationship, however, their new looks are what got the most attention!

    "Don’t just marry someone you can live with — Marry someone you cannot live without!" Joy-Anna captioned the selfie which showed off her new dark bangs. 

    Yes, bangs! And not gonna lie, she is giving us major Jamie Sullivan vibes. 

    "I love you, my mountain Man! #hesmybestfriend #soooblessed #imarriedup #noshavenovember #livinglifetothefullest" she wrote alongside a black heart and kissing emoji. 

    Joy-Anna’s siblings immediately chimed in on her hair change, which mind you, is a pretty big deal in the Duggar household thanks to their strict religion and legendary rules. 

    "The bangs look great! Joy, you are so beautiful inside and out!" wrote Anna Duggar. 

    Jinger Duggar added, "Love the bangs, Joy joy!" to which Jill Duggar echoed, "Cute joy joy!" 

    Some fans thought that in addition to the new cut Joy-Anna had darkened her hair color, but she answered in the comment section, writing "No, I’ve actually [never] dyed/highlighted my hair! it’s probably the lighting!"

    The mother of one also posted the same image to her Instagram Story, which she captioned, "I love you, my mountain Man!" #noshavenovember #newbangs."

    But Joy-Anna wasn’t the only one rocking a new look — her "mountain man" smiled for the photo as he proudly showed off his *in the works* beard.

    Their new styles compliment each other quite nicely, I must say. 

    Joy-Anna’s new cut comes just five months after her older sister Jinger debuted her new ‘do!

    This past June, Jinger revealed that she had ditched her brunette locks as she showed off her new brighter and lighter look — blonde hair! 

    "Decided to go with the blonde balayage this Summer" she captioned the post with a smiley emoji.

    We’re glad to see that Joy-Anna and Austin are doing well and lookin’ great as they still recover from their tragic miscarriage back in July.

    The couple told Us Weekly earlier this month that they are waiting but will be trying for another baby soon. 

    "We want to be wise and give my body time to heal," the 22-year-old reality star said. 

    "But we are excited for the children that God’s going to give us and can’t wait. I don’t know if four is the number. I mean, I would be fine with a few more if that’s what God wants us to have."

    Well, it seems that they are more than ready to grow their family considering they just bought a 3-bedroom, 3-bath house!

    (Which, BTW, they spent only $10 on…) 

    Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar originally owned the home. So, it is in the family name. So, technically we should say they *got* a new home, but not my business. 

    "Sooo… WE BOUGHT A HOUSE and YES! We plan to settle down and live in this one for awhile! Joy-Anna wrote to Instagram.

    "After a year and a half of camper living we’re so ready!!!"

    We’re very excited to watch Joy-Anna and Austin raise their future kiddos in their lovely new home on TLC!
