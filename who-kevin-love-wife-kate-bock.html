    KEVIN Love and his wife Kate Bock tied the knot in June 2022.

    The couple met first in 2016 in New York City, making it an easy decision when it came time to decide where to hold their wedding.

    Who is Kevin Love's wife Kate Bock?

    Kate Bock is an international swimsuit model best known for her time with Sports Illustrated.

    Bock has appeared in Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue since 2013 and shared the cover in 2020.

    In her career, Bock has also appeared on the cover of Maxim magazine and been featured in several issues of Elle.

    Outside of magazines, Bock has also modeled as an angel for Victoria's Secret.

    Bock was discovered when she was 12 years old in Vancouver, Canada.

    After she finished high school, Bock moved to Paris, where she became fluent in French.

    When did Kevin Love and Kate Bock get married?

    On Saturday, June 25, 2022, the model and the Cleveland Cavaliers player said "I do" at the New York Public Library.

    But not before they shared coffee and got ready for the big day at the St Regis hotel, where they got coffee together for their first date.

    Every detail, from Bock's custom Ralph Lauren wedding dress inspired by Grace Kelly to the wedding favors (custom Nike Air Force 1s), was meant to make a lasting impression.

    The couple danced their first song to Sam Cooke's That's Where It's At.

    Does Kevin Love play for the Cleveland Cavaliers?

    As of February 16, 2023, Kevin Love still plays for the Cleveland Cavalier NBA franchise.

    However, that could soon be changing.

    Love was an integral part of Cleveland's 2016 championship team and is now the only major player from that season still with the team.

    Despite his winning record, he hasn't been in the Cavs' rotation for the past 12 games.

    His management reportedly requested a buyout of the remainder of his 4-year, $120million contract.
