    The model told the outlet that her husband "loves the boobs too," saying: "He's got one side, [Chanel's] got the other. She's going to be a boob freak."

    "We call it the family bed. We have all the dogs, Chanel, that's our bonding time together.

    "We incorporate our family bed every single night. We co-sleep and that's another one people want to get on," she explained.

    "It's a weird situation because I don't know why I have to explain myself."

    'BONDING EXPERIENCE'

    Although Coco has clapped back at haters who disagree with breastfeeding past age two, she said she is "not really an arguer."

    "I just don't believe society says they should stop at two," she asserted.

    "You as a mother should stop because they said so. I'm [not] going to stop because I feel like it's a great bonding experience between her and I. 

    "She's my only child, it's not like I have a big family of kids, and so I'm savoring every moment with this child."
