    Lea Michele &amp; Zandy Reich: Married!!

    Lea Michele is officially a married woman!! The formerGlee star officially tied the knot with Zandy Reich on Saturday in an intimate and small ceremony in Northern California, according to People.

    09 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Lea Michele is officially a married woman!! 

    The former Glee star officially tied the knot with Zandy Reich on Saturday in an intimate and small ceremony in Northern California, according to People. 

    "We are so excited to be married and grateful to be surrounded by our friends and family. And most of all, we’re so happy to spend the rest of our lives together," the couple gushed to the outlet. 

    Lea and Zandy wedded after two years of dating and almost a year following their engagement.

    Their romance was publically confirmed after the two were spotted holding hands in New York City back in July of 2017. 

    "They have been friends for a while and started dating recently." a source told E! News at the time. 

    Lea’s relationship with the 36-year-old is pretty unique, as he is actually outside of the entertainment industry. 

    In fact, he sounds completely normal. 

    He graduated with a bachelor’s degree in economics from the University of Pennsylvania and also attended UPenn’s esteemed Wharton School of Business. 

    In April of 2018, the 32-year-old actress confirmed her engagement to Zandy, who is now the president of clothing brand AYR. 

    Showing off her massive studded rock to Instagram, Lea simply captioned the shot, "Yes."

    The singer explained how surprised she was to People saying, "I think the first thing I said to him was, ‘stop joking around!’ Everyone said, ‘Try to be present’ because it happens so quickly." 

    "Which I didn’t listen to at all and blacked out and I barely remember. We both blacked out and were like, ‘What? Did we say yes? Is this happening?’" 

    A source told E! that Zandy proposed to Lea with a 4-carat elongated, radiant-cut ring, which he exclusively designed with Leor Yerushalmi and The Jewelers of Las Vegas. 

    That same insider also added that the newly engaged duo "couldn’t be happier." 

    Lea and Zandy enjoyed an engagement party a few months later alongside famous friends Ashley Tisdale, Hilary Duff, Becca Tobin, and Billie Lourd. 

    "The outpouring of love from our friends and family who showed up for us today and threw us the most epic and beautiful engagement party we could have ever asked for was truly so incredible," Lea shared on her Instagram story.

    "Z and I love you all and are forever grateful." 

    One month ago the celebration continued in Hawaii as Lea took a trip with her besties Emma Roberts, Jamie-Lynn Singler and JoAnna Garcia Swisher for her bachelorette party. 

    And this past Saturday, on March 9, the time finally came for the love birds to become Mr. and Mrs! 

    At the wedding, the couple partied with their family and close friends, of course including some of Lea’s special Glee pals. 

    We can only imagine that lots of beautiful and joyous singing took place throughout the special occasion. 

    Congratulations to the newlyweds!
