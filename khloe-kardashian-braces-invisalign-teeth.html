    Khloe Kardashian Gets Braces — Chooses Invisalign Over Metal Braces

    On May 9, Khloe tweeted: “Thank you @drkevinsands for being the best dentist ever! If only we could all be put to sleep like Lamar LOL maybe next time :)”

    Her husband Lamar Odom has shown his fear of the dentist on their reality show before, and he has to be put to sleep for any treatment he receives!

    Dr. Sands Speaks On Invisalign

    Dr. Sands spoke to us EXCLUSIVELY about Invisalign — find out more about the treatment used by celebs like Kim Kardashian, Emma Stone, Faith Hill, Tom Cruise and more!

    When is a person a good candidate for Invisalign?

    “Anyone who wants to improve their smile — like braces for crooked teeth, rotated teeth or gaps or a bad bites. It does the same as traditional braces except it is less noticeable,” Dr. Sands tells us.

    If Khloe has straight teeth, what is the benefit of Invisalign?

    “She had almost straight teeth — a little crooked — she wanted to perfect them. It’s Invisalign express — just a few trays to get them perfect. A minor case.”

    What is the timing compared to traditional braces?

    “Invisalign usually takes longer because of less patient compliance — patients usually wear the trays 23 hours a day and braces are on your teeth 24 hours a day.”

    What is the cost compared to traditional braces?

    “Invisalign is twice as expensive — there are more of them to make. The average cost for Invisalign is $3, 000 – 7,000, metal braces are $2,000 – 5,000.”

    Dr. Sands is based in Beverly Hills. If you’re on the other side of the country, we love going to Lavaan Dental Spa in New York City. The ultra-chic office is luxurious and friendly and only offers teeth whitening and Invisalign, so there are no scary drilling sounds.
