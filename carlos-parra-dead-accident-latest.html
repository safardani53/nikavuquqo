    More on Carlos Parra's tragic death

    The singer Carlos Parra has been killed in a horror car accident, his bandmates have said.

    Parra, who was part of the Phoenix-based band Los Parras, died on Saturday at the age of 26.

    On Sunday, the band posted on Instagram: “To all the friends, family, and fans of the group, with a broken heart we want to inform you our brother, Carlos Parra, died in a car accident yesterday. RIP little brother, we love you.”

    They shared lyrics from their hit track "Por verte feliz," which translates to "To see you happy" as part of their tribute.

    It's not known where the accident occurred.

    Eduin Caz pays tribite

    The Mexican singer Eduin Caz offered his condolences to the band and said: "May God have him in his glory. I send you a hug and a lot of strength."

    And, the artist Edgardo Nuñez posted: "Rest in peace Carlitos."

    The renowned music producer Pepe Garza said: "What a tragedy."

    Singer's friend pays tribute following tragic passing

    Following the death of Carlos Parra, tributes have continued to pour in.

    His pal Jose Brito told the CBS affiliate KPHO: “He was a happy guy, he was always smiling, and he was always motivated to do something for his family.”

    Brito said he had known the siblings for 16 years and he went to high school with them.

    He described Carlos as "humble" and said he had recently got engaged.
