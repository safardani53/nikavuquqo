    NETFLIX'S You is currently in its third season and stars Victoria Pedretti and Dylan Arnold.

    Outside of the show, fans are curious if their off-screen chemistry is just as strong as what they've seen this season.

    Are You stars Victoria Pedretti and Dylan Arnold dating?

    In this season of You, Pedretti's, 26, character, Love Quinn, is seen getting involved in an affair with 19-year-old college Theo Engler, who is played by Dylan Arnold, 27.

    Once fans saw their on-screen romance, they began to wonder if sparks were flying in real life.

    While the stars have not commented on the rumored romance, it has been reported that the two are actually dating.

    In the past, both stars have kept their relationship status as private as possible so many fans aren't surprised that the answer to this question remains unclear.

    What did Victoria Pedretti say about filming intimate scenes this season?

    Following the release of season 3, Pedretti, opened up about filming the intimate scenes with her on-screen husband Penn Badgley, who plays Joe Goldberg.

    "This is not a show where you can be like, 'Nobody's going to kiss. It'll be fine.' So it took months to figure out how to do it safely," Pedretti told Entertainment Weekly.

    "I felt very lucky that they start off their relationship at odds with each other, navigating the awkwardness of being new parents, because it felt strange to be physically intimate very early on, even though we're supposed to be a married couple."

    Badgley later added:  "It's really true, the implications of all that stuff was really felt. There were significant rewrites throughout the whole season to try and negotiate around these scenes that would require a lot of people."

    How can I watch Victoria Pedretti and Dylan Arnold in You?

    While Pedretti is a returning cast member, Arnold is new this season.

    Fans can stream all three season of You exclusively on Netflix.

    At this time, it remains unclear when season 4 will be released but it was confirmed just one day prior to the season 3 release.
