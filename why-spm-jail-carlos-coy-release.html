    Who is Carlos Coy?

    Carlos Coy, 51, is an American rapper and record producer.

    He was born on October 5, 1970, in the South Park neighborhood of Houston, Texas.

    Throughout his early life, Coy reportedly worked as a drug dealer and was in and out of school.

    The High So High singer released his debut album, Hillwood, in 1995.

    What does SPM stand for?

    SPM stands for South Park Mexican.
