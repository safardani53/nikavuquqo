    A CLASSIC 90s Ford is up for sale - but it comes with an eye-watering price.

    The 1996 Ford Escort Cosworth Lux is just one of just over 100 of the motor produced.

    The coveted vehicle has clocked up a meagre 16,000 miles and will be up for grabs for whoever coughs up the jaw-dropping £89,995 asking fee.

    This example boasts a squeaky-clean cabin, black brake callipers in similarly optimal condition, as well as an "excellent panel fit and finish".

    Initially designed to to qualify as a Group A car for the World Rally Championship between 1993 and 1998, the 'rally' version of the Ford Escort was made available to the public in limited numbers.

    Sellers Car and Classic say the motor "has been used sparingly with a mere 16k miles covered and presents in superb condition."
