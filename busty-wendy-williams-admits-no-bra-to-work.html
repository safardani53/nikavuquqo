    A BUSTY Wendy Williams admitted she sometimes doesn't wear a BRA to work.

    However, the daytime talk show host said she puts one on before the show because she "doesn't want to offend."

    Wendy said: "Today is National No Bra Day. It's in support of Breast Cancer awareness. I don't know that a bra has to do with breast cancer. But all I know is after my wig, off goes the bra.

    "Now I wear a bra on the show cause I don't want to offend you."

    She went on: "A lot of women don't wear bras, and I get it. For me when I wake up in the morning and I come to work, I don't wear a bra. I just throw on a negligee or a t-shirt and some leggings.

    "But I only do that when it's jacket and sweater season cause I don't want to offend the streets... But for you A-cups you can do it every day, all day."

    Last month Wendy said during the Hot Topics segment of her show: “By the way, what I forgot to tell you guys yesterday when I was talking about those 25 pounds is that my doctor because I said how much do my boobs weigh?

    "So, he goes, ‘Like three pounds a piece.'

    “Well, I will get a reduction when I’d like to. But right now, I’m still having fun."

    Wendy also revealed on the show's season 12 premiere: "I've lost 25 lbs... And I didn't do it on purpose. It's just that food became disgusting to me.

    "You know I was cooking at first, and it was all good. I'm shoveling stuff in my mouth.

    "I'm going out to eat... everything from hot dogs to lobster, king crab legs all that stuff. But then it became like I'm done with food."
