    Where is David Venable today?

    David Venable's show, In the Kitchen with David, airs on QVC twice a week, on Wednesdays at 8 p.m. ET and Sundays at noon.

    David was reported missing from his regularly programmed show on September 11, 2022.

    However, according to the QVC program guide, David's show was actually aired on QVC2, instead.

    This is not the first time David's show has been moved due to special programming.

    Previously on March 6, The Sun sent a request to QVC for comment on the foodie's disappearance from his show on Sunday afternoon and received confirmation about his whereabouts the following day.

    According to a QVC spokesperson, "This past weekend was an all-fashion event on the main QVC channel, so David’s show aired on QVC2."

    Adding: "We occasionally move or change our static shows to accommodate special programming themes – as was the case last weekend."

    Who is David Venable?

    David, 57, is a TV personality and author who has hosted his show on QVC since 2009.

    He was hired by QVC in 1993 and at first, promoted the company's gourmet food products.

    David gradually earned the right to host his own show on the channel after a few years.

    David is not married, nor does he have any reported children.

    He was born on November 12, 1964, in Charlotte, North Carolina, and is a proud alumnus of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he studied journalism.
