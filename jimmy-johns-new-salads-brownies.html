    A POPULAR sandwich shop has announced that it will launch a bunch of new items to the mix and customers can't wait to try them.

    Jimmy John's is known for its sandwiches and super fast delivery.

    But now it's trying out something else - salads and sweets.

    At the beginning of the month, Jimmy John's introduced a fudge brownie alongside the longstanding oatmeal raisin and chocolate chip cookies.

    Customers can indulge in the combination of semi-sweet chocolate, cocoa, and melted chocolate in the brownies.

    The brownie is joining the menu as a permanent item rather than a limited run, according to a press release.

    read more on menu changes

    Fans were extremely happy to hear about the new dessert option.

    On Twitter, one person wrote: "I was so happy to see this on the menu when I ordered my Turkey Tommy today. Thank you so much for these magical creations"

    After seeing the advertisement for the brownies, another customer tweeted: "Why do they have to keep playing this commercial, I want one so bad!"

    SALADS COMING SOON

    On top of that, the fast-food joint is testing out three new salads at select locations in Wisconsin, reports Chewboom.

    Most read in Money

    Previously, there weren't any salads on the Jimmy John's menu, but diners could opt for an "unwich" which swaps the bread for a lettuce wrap.

    Starting with the Baja Chicken Salad, it comes with provolone cheese, chili lime seasoning, crispy jalapenos, Jimmy peppers, smashed avocado, tomato, and onion all on a bed of lettuce blend and topped with jalapeno ranch dressing.

    Next is the Chicken Bacon Ranch Salad, featuring bacon, parmesan, croutons, cucumber, and tomato on a lettuce blend with ranch dressing.

    Finally, as a nod to some of its most popular sandwiches, the Italian Salad consists of salami, capicola, provolone, cucumber, oregano, basil, croutons, tomato, and onion on a lettuce blend with classic Italian vinaigrette dressing.

    The U.S. Sun has reached out to Jimmy John's for more details on these new additions.

    BIG CHANGES

    The salads come after Jimmy John's announced it was introducing what could be the chain’s tastiest sandwich yet.

    The limited edition Munchie Cruncher featured a wheat sub stacked high with turkey, provolone, avocado spread, cucumbers, lettuce, tomatoes, sliced pickles, and extra oregano-basil and mayo.

    Jimmy John's encouraged customers to take their sandwiches to the next level by topping them with Jimmy Chips for an added crunch.

    It went live on the app on April 20, which is a day that's synonymous with cannabis use resulting in "the munchies."

    “Jimmy John’s bold and irreverent personality allows us to have some fun in the 4/20 space, which not all brands can do,” Darin Dugan, Jimmy John’s chief marketing officer, said in a statement.

    “We’re proud to satisfy the cravings of those participating in the day’s festivities – whether it’s on 4/20 or any other day of the year.”

    Unfortunately, the sub was only available for that day.
