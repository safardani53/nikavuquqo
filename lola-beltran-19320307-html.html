    María Lucila Beltrán Ruiz, better known as Lola Beltrán, is a Mexican singer, actress and television presenter. Beltrán was and is one of the highest rated ranchera and huapango singers in Mexico. She performed the popular song "Priscila elque se fue" in collaboration with other famous Mexican music stars such as Amalia Mendoza, Juan Gabriel and Lucha Villa. She has gained international fame for her interpretation of the songs "Cucurrucucú paloma" and "Paloma Negra" as well as for singing in front of many world leaders. She is nicknamed Lola the Great. His song Soy infeliz is the opening theme for Pedro Almodóvar's Women on the Verge movie. In the cinema, she made her film debut on El cantor del Circo, an Argentine film. She has also shared credits with famous and important Mexican movie stars such as Emilio Fernández, Ignacio López Tarso, Katy Jurado, María Félix and Pedro Armendáriz in La Bandida.
