    KENDALL Jenner has split fans after she debuted her Halloween cucumber costume in a new video.

    The 26-year-old took to TikTok to show off the costume, which was deemed hilarious by some, and not so much by others.

    The costume attempts to clap back at her cucumber-related meme that went viral during the first season of The Kardashians.

    Captioned with just a "knife" and a "cucumber" emoji, the video played audio taken from the film Mean Girls as the Hulu star modeled the oversized cucumber slice.

    Kendall first appeared wearing skintight latex leggings, their dark green color meant to match the shiny skin of a cucumber.

    Her arms stayed tucked inside the torso-sized costume as she rocked side to side.

    Later in the clip, and now with her arms freed, Kendall waved around a butcher knife as she side-eyed the camera.

    Fans reposted the TikTok on a Kardashian-dedicated Reddit board where they could weigh in on Kendall's ironic costume choice.

    "Oh look at her having a sense of humor! Now If she went as a Pepsi can I’d scream," one person wrote referring to the now infamous 2017 soda ad.

    Another fan wrote: "Okay. This is actually the best thing ever. Glad to see she can laugh at herself."

    A user added: "Respect, Kendall."

    "I love that she did this and is still awkwardly holding that knife," added a fourth with another person commenting: "I love it."

    One commenter expressed their appreciation for the in-joke but suspected others wouldn't be so accepting, writing "I think it’s silly-funny but I know some people are gonna hate for it."

    They were correct as one Twitter user posted: "Kendall really thought she did something with that cucumber and knife costume- just to prove a point- it’s giving stupidity and lame."

    IN ON THE JOKE

    This isn't the first time Kendall mocked herself for her vegetable-slicing method as she posted a cucumber photo soon after she was originally slammed for the incident.

    Her Instagram Story post showcased a cucumber and a kitchen knife sitting on top of a colorful cutting board. 

    The cucumber had been sliced at both ends. 

    Kendall captioned the post: "Here we go again." 

    The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum has been endlessly mocked by fans and family ever since her episode aired.

    'CUCUMBER GIRL'

    During The Kardashians, Kendall's mother, Kris Jenner, 66, knew that she lacked the skills to hold a blade.

    When Kendall started chopping, she held the vegetable on the side and placed her fingers mere millimeters from the blade's sharp edge.

    She then awkwardly crossed her arms, instead of rotating the vegetable.

    When the clip found its way onto TikTok, critics jumped to the comments section and slammed the way she cut the cucumber.

    One mentioned at the time: "Always knew they were so far removed from reality but watching Kendall try to cut that cucumber was painful."

    Even sister Kylie, 25, had to join in on the fun and mock her supermodel sister.

    It all kicked off as Kendall and Kylie danced together while celebrating sibling Kourtney Kardashian, 43, and Travis Barker's wedding. 

    Kylie sipped a cocktail while her sibling danced and burst into song.

    Before the video concluded, she placed her glass of tequila in front of the camera as she cheered with her followers.

    Kylie captioned the post: "Me and cucumber girl."

    MISTRESS OF THE DARK

    As for Kylie, the youngest Jenner recently outdid herself this Halloween after she transformed into Elvira: Mistress of the Dark.

    Kylie took to TikTok on Sunday night to share a seriously sexy video of her latest costume, which certainly emphasized her cleavage.

    As the song vent by rapper Baby Keem played, the reality TV star pouted for the camera as it zoomed in and out.

    Kylie highlighted her chest in a black velvet plunging dress which just about covered her modesty.

    She sported some heavy black winged eyeliner and bright red lips as well as a dramatic beehive hairdo.

    Her fans were delighted with the video and quickly commented on it.
