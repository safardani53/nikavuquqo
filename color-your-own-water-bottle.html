    Ideas for Decorating and Coloring Your Own Water Bottle

    You can decorate your water bottle to inject your style and uniqueness into it with paper cutouts, paints, stickers, and add-ons. Here are a few ideas you can share with your kids when they’re using the crafting kit for water bottles:

    Ribbon stripes

    Give the bottle a pop of color and a nice texture with strands of ribbon. Different ribbon widths, colors, and dimensions you apply the stripes in can create a nice design. You can overlap wide and narrow ribbon stripes, or simply round up stripes around the body to add design interest. Save ribbon from water damage by applying decoupage glue on top.

    Collage

    Let your kids collect cutouts from their favorite magazines and photos and use them on the water bottle to create a collage. Use decoupage glue to attach the cutouts.

    Glass paints

    Glass paints transform your bottle to look just like a stained glass window. Create lead lines with permanent markers on your bottle and paint into them. Liquid leading can also be used for this purpose. You’ll have to use a clear paint sealer on top or spray a little polyurethane spray to make the paint stay.

    Vinyl stickers

    Vinyl stickers come in a huge variety. Vinyl stickers are completely waterproof and are great for bottles you take along on your outdoor activities such as hikes and camping. If you’re using paper stickers, make sure you enamel them with decoupage glue to protect them.

    How to Pick a Water Bottle Coloring Craft Kit

    Here are a few considerations you should keep in mind when buying a water bottle for coloring:

    Ease of use

    Make sure that the bottle is easy to use for your kid. This includes the weight, height, and bottle cover. A water bottle can weigh from 9 to 16 ounces. Pick a bottle that your child can decorate and take along to school, it should be able to fit into lunch boxes and small hands.

    The cover should securely seal the bottle but should be easy to open and close. If you’re picking a bottle for a young (2-3-year-old) child, prefer getting a bottle with straw spouts as that’s easy for kids to operate.

    Easy to clean

    Since kids like to play and get things dirty, the bottle you pick for them should be easy to clean. It’s better if the bottle has certain parts that can be taken apart to clean and wash separately. You can check to see if the bottle is dishwasher safe. Avoid getting bottles with tight crevices as that’s a favored spot for mold and mildew to grow.

    Leak-proof

    Look for a bottle that has a rubber seal in its cup to prevent spills and leaks to the most extent. You can check to see the closure type, and read customer reviews about the product before purchasing.

    Material

    Crafting kit water bottles are made from stainless steel or plastic. Stainless steel bottles might seem a bit heavy for small kids in comparison to plastic ones. However, when buying a plastic coloring water bottle, make sure that you’re completely satisfied with the safety of plastic used. It should be BPA and toxins-free.

    Decorations

    The quality of the decorations or colors in the color of your own water bottle kit should be considered to avoid unnecessary and premature wear. The paints and decorative items used should stick to the bottle securely.

    Buying Tips for Color Your Own Water Bottle Kits

    Some tips to follow:

    • Ensure that the bottle decorating kit you buy is age-appropriate. Small beads and decorative craft items can be hazardous for small children.
    • Do not purchase an advanced skill level kit for a two-year-old as they can accidentally swallow small items.
    • Look for kits that are educational and stimulate thinking in the child. Turn their playtime into a learning experience. Get a kit with alphabets, shapes, and lots of items for them to experiment with.

    Benefits of Water Bottle Coloring Crafts for Kids

    Coloring and designing your own water bottle is a great activity for kids and even adults. Color your own water bottle kits for children help them engage with colors, shapes, ideas and help them translate their thoughts into shapes and figures on an object. Here are a few proven benefits:

    • It develops the fine motor skills of a child as they work on delicate lines and boundaries.
    • Helps in enhancing and improving their imagination and teaches freedom of expression.
    • BPA-free construction makes sure that the bottle is safe for children to use and play with.
    • Rhinestones, beads, and sequins for decorating the bottle help develop better concentration and focus in kids as they work on a bottle.
    • These kits will reveal creators and artists. The choice of colors and techniques they use reveals their individuality.
    • Such craft projects are great for all-rounded early development.
    • Keeps kids busy and away from boredom for hours.
    • They are a great option for parties, birthdays, sleepovers, and get together.

    Conclusion

    Water bottle craft kits are made from safe, BPA-free plastics and come with all the tools for your kid to create and personalize their bottles. This is an excellent activity to keep their minds healthy and creative. This guide will help you pick the finest color your own water bottles in 2022.
