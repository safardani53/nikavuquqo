    Creator and founder of CoInspire, Lilia Luciano, is a talented television journalist and a filmmaker. She has also established herself as a public speaker. She is also famous for being the news correspondent of NBC News's The Today Show, Nightly News, and so on.

    TEDx speaker Lilia was born as Lilia Luciano in San Juan, Puerto Rico, on the 12th of October 1984. She was also raised there. After she completed her high school graduation, she moved to Massachusetts. She is American.

    Though she is already 31, she has not been involved in love affairs, and it is also believed that she has not been married yet. She is still single.

    Lilia got enrolled at Tufts University to learn pre-medicine. But later, she became interested in journalism, and then she joined the University of Miami in 2003. She finally earned her bachelor's degree in economics and broadcast journalism in 2007. While she was enrolled at the University of Miami, she took an internship at Telemundo.

    During those days, she worked as a producer and anchor of a finance and economics segment. After she earned her undergraduate degree, she joined Univision Networks and worked as a correspondent and co-anchor of the show, En Vivo y Directo. She was later hired by NBC News in 2010. Her net worth is expected to be around a million.
