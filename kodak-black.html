    Who is Kodak Black?

    Bill Kahan Kapri, better known by his stage name Kodak Black, is an American rapper.

    He gained initial recognition with his single “No Flockin”, released in 2014. His debut album, Painting Pictures, peaked at number three on the US Billboard 200 and included the Billboard Hot 100 top 10 single “Tunnel Vision”.

    Kodak Black Career

    Kodak Black started his career at a very young age. He began rapping at the age of six and also changed his nickname to Lil Black.

    Kodak Black released his debut album in the year 2017, named Painting Pictures. It received some critical acclaim and peaked at number  3 on the billboards.
