    The reality television series Growing Up Hip Hop premiered in 2016 and centered on the offspring of hip-hop royalty.

    The original cast featured Master P's son Romeo Miller, Angela Simmons, the daughter of Rev Run, Kristinia DeBarge (of DeBarge fame), Damon Dash's son Boogie Dash, Egypt Criss, the daughter of Pepa (of Salt-n-Pepa), and TJ Mizell, Jam Master Jay's son.

    Supporting cast members over the years has included Bow Wow, Andre King (Swizz Beatz's brother), and Kyndall Ferguson. Growing Up Hip Hop has two spinoffs: Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta and Growing Up Hip Hop: New York.

    Network: WE tv

    First Episode Date: January 7, 2016
