    What is Rhona Mitra doing Now?

    Rhona Mitra is an English actress, model, and singer who is well-acclaimed for her American debut role as Holly Marie (1999-2000) in the television series, Party of Five. She rose to fame as the Lara Croft model (1997-1998) before Angelina Jolie left her legacy as the Tomb Raider character.

    Since then, the Paddington, Hampstead native has had numerous memorable roles in television such as Dr. Alejandra Ollie Klein on Gideon's Crossing (2000-2001), lawyer Tara Wilson on The Practice (2003-2004) and Boston Legal (2004), vampire wife Claire Radcliff on The Gates (2010), Maj. Rachel Dalton on Strike Back (2012-2013), and Dr. Rachel Scott on The Last Ship (2014-2015).

    In 2018, she was announced as a cast member on the CW show, Supergirl, to portray the role of a villainess, Mercy Graves- who is Lex Luthor's longtime bodyguard.

    Rhona Mitra's Short Bio: How Old is She?

    Mitra was born on 9 August 1976 (age 46), in Paddington, Hampstead, London, as the daughter of Anthony Mitra, a cosmetic surgeon, and Nora Downey. 

    She possesses a mixed ethnicity of Indian-Irish from her Bengali Indian descent father and Irish mother.

    At the age of eight, in 1984, her parents divorced. Because of that, she was sent to boarding school. Spending several years at two different schools, including Roedean, she was eventually expelled from both of them after creating considerable troubles in her adolescence.
