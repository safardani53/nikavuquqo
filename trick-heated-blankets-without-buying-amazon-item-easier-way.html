    A HOME decor enthusiast has shared her hack to warm herself up within minutes that doesn't break the bank.

    She told viewers about an Amazon buy that is one of her new favorite additions to her home, but commenters have suggested an even simpler trick.

    Chelsea Hope (@itschelseahope) is a self-described "virtual BFF for home finds," according to her TikTok bio.

    She told viewers about a fast way to warm your blankets this winter season using an Amazon item in a video.

    Chelsea first happily sat on her bed and wrapped herself in an oversized Marshall's blanket with a smile on her face.

    "When you figure out how to have heated blankets without buying heated ones, " the subtitle read.

    She then showed viewers how she shoved it into a white circular bucket warmer in her home.

    With the press of a button, she turned the simple homeware item on and off.

    It took her blanket from room temp to cozy.

    Chelsea dubbed her large towel warmer a "must-have" for making all of her favorite blankets heated.
