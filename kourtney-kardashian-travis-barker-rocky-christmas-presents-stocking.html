    KOURTNEY Kardashian and Travis Barker have flaunted their newborn son Rocky's extravagant gifts and personalized stocking for his first Christmas in new photos.

    The couple recently shared the first photos of their baby boy after keeping him away from the limelight.

    On Monday, Kourtney, 44, took to her Instagram Stories to post a snap of Rocky's stocking with Santa Clause on it and the youngster's name stitched on the top.

    It matched his parents' stockings, which Kourtney showed in another pic of them hanging with candy canes sticking out.

    Later in the night, Travis, 48, posted a snap on his page of his youngest child's stack of presents, including stuffed animals and toys under a white teepee with Christmas lights draped over it.

    At the top was a Santa hat with Rocky's name on it, showing that the dozens of gifts were for the nearly 2-month-old.

    Kourtney Kardashian

    The posts came hours after Kourtney shared a snap of Rocky donning festive elf slippers while celebrating Christmas Eve.

    Travis also posted a photo of custom baby boxer shorts with Rocky Barker monogrammed on the waistband and pockets.

    The Blink-182 drummer previously shared that his son's moniker was partly inspired by the hit 1976 boxing film Rocky, so the present was fitting for the tot.

    FIRST PHOTOS

    On Friday, Kourtney and Travis pleasantly surprised fans when they gave their followers a first glimpse at their new bundle of joy.

    One Instagram photo showed The Kardashians star with her head in her rocker husband's lap as he held their baby close to his chest.

    A second shot captured the little one's feet while being cradled by his father.

    And a third highlighted a special moment between Kourtney and her son as she breastfed him.

    Two other photos had fans gushing as they showed Travis kissing Rocky's cheek and the tot lying on his father's chest.

    "Rocky!!!!!" Kourtney's sister, Khloe Kardashian, 39, wrote in the comments with heart and happy face emojis.

    "Welcome to the world, Rocky! Sending you love Kourtney and Travis! What a beautiful little Christmas miracle!" said a fan.

    "They look very happy and deep in the whole parenting and couple thing. Love this!" a second user added.

    "You guys are so full of love congratulations," commented a third.

    BLENDED FAMILY

    Kourtney and Travis welcomed their son on November 1 and hadn't introduced him to the public until now.

    The baby's full name is Rocky Thirteen Barker, which Travis confirmed in December.

    Kourtney has also remained out of the spotlight until recently when she posted a video of herself easing back into her workouts.

    She also appeared at the Kardashian-Jenner family's annual Christmas Eve bash and shared highlights from the over-the-top party online.

    Rocky is Kourtney and Travis' first child together.
