    The latest man Charli D’Amelio has been linked to is Landon Barker, Travis Barker's son. Here's their relationship timeline from the start.

    The Gist:

    • Charli D'Amelio and Landon Barker started dating in June 2022.
    • Landon has a tattoo of Charli's eye on his arm.
    • The couple is still going strong in 2023 and Charli appeared in the music video for Landon's highly-anticipated single, "Friends With Your Ex."

    Over the years, TikTok has been instrumental in the life and career of Charli D’Amelio. Since so many people pay attention to her and the viral dancing videos she posts on her TikTok feed, a lot of people also care about her relationship status. The latest man Charli has been romantically linked to is none other than Landon Barker.

    If you think you already recognize his name and face, you’d be right. He’s the son of famous drummer Travis Barker. Here’s what fans should know about Charli and Landon's blossoming romance.

    Charli D’Melio and Landon Barker's relationship timeline kicks off in June 2022.

    Rumors about Charli and Landon were first sparked after she attended his show in early June 2022. They were spotted leaving the concert together afterward –– and it caused quite the stir. A source told People, “They’re seeing each other, and it’s early stages.”

    When Charli and Landon both posted pictures of their new tattoos, the rumor mill began spinning even faster.

    The reason why? Apparently, they went to the same tattoo artist to get work done. A couple of days after that, Charli and Landon were seen dipping away from Dixie D’Amelio's album party on their own. However, the official confirmation of the coupledom came in July 2022 with Landon posting a video of the pair vibing to music on TikTok, per People. Charli followed suit with an Instagram post of the pair kissing.

    Landon shaded Charli's ex, Chade Hudson, in July 2022.

    After Chase Hudson aka Lil Huddy threw shade at Charli in the song "All the Things I Hate About You," Landon decided to return the same energy and throw shade with an Instagram Story.

    "If you can't treat her right I'll treat her right for you 7/23," the text overlay read with a photo of him kissing Charli.

    In August 2022, Chase's buddy Bryce Hall also threw shade at Charli by sharing a teaser from the song.

    "Hey same @charlidamelio," with the prompt "put your finger down to what applies to you," in the caption.

    Again, Chase clapped back by making a duet video and not labeling Charli as a "home hopper."

    Charli gushed about Landon during an interview in September 2022.

    Charli was a contestant on ABC's Dancing With the Stars Season 31. After her debut performance that earned positive reviews, she talked to Us Weekly about how much Landon had supported her.

    "He was very proud of me," Charli said. "He's never seen me dance before, so I think he was nervous. But he's always been so supportive."

    In a September 2022 interview on the BFFs podcast with her older sister Dixie, the siblings chatted about how she met Landon and it's all due to Dixie. Dixie revealed that she "set it up."

    "I just saw him and was like, 'Why don't you talk to Landon? He seems like a nice kid,'" Dixie said. "Your type, I guess. And I didn't think any more of it, I was being a good older sister."

    Charli agreed and gave listeners some of the scoop on the relationship.

    "What happened is [that] for these past two years, I've put what everyone thinks of me above my own happiness and this was the first time that I really listened to my heart," the social media influencer explained. "If that creates drama for other people, that's theirs to deal with. I'm happy. He's happy and that's really all to it."

    In October 2022, Charli shared a touching birthday tribute post to Landon where she called him "landlord."

    In the first half of 2023, Charli and Landon attended industry events together and worked with one another.

    Charli and Landon set the red carpet on fire at the 2023 Grammy Awards. The pair then attended Milan Fashion Week to support Kendall Jenner who walked the runway.

    A few months later, Charli and Landon graced the cover of Mini V together in April 2023. In the joint interview, Landon talked about the qualities he admires most in a woman while giving his main squeeze a nod.

    "Humor for sure," Landon said. "I'm constantly cracking jokes and I love being around people that do the same! A quality I especially admire about Charli."

    On May 1, 2023, Landon shared a beautiful tribute to Charli on her birthday.

    Landon then upped the ante by getting a tattoo of Charli's eye on his arm the following month.

    Charli appeared in Landon's music video for the song "Friends With Your Ex" and are still going strong.

    On Sept. 22, 2023, Landon released the music video for his highly-anticipated single, "Friends With Your Ex" and of course, Charli made an appearance. In the video, Landon accidentally fell in love with Charli, who was dating one of his bandmates.

    The couple is still going strong and has grown as one of social media's favorite couples. In fact, Charli took to Instagram on Oct. 20, 2023, to share a sweet birthday tribute for Landon.

    Young love, isn't it grand?
