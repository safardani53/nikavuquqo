    Mike Sorrentino talks about his ‘big Italian family’

    Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino is busier than ever, with a reality TV show airing, and two kids at home.

    In an interview with E! News, Mike talked about his dream of having a “big Italian family” and how crazy his days are.

    “Me and my wife sort of divide and conquer,” he said.

    “The main word or the key word I’d have to say is definitely ‘survive’ on a daily basis, but we wouldn’t have it any other way. I’ve always wanted a big Italian family and we’re definitely off to a good start.”

    How Ronnie and Sammi kept busy after Jersey Shore

    Ronnie Ortiz-Magro and Samantha ‘Sammi Sweetheart’ Giancola famously dated on Jersey Shore.

    Neither officially joined the cast for the spinoff series, but have been keeping busy with other endeavors.

    Over the years, Ronnie has appeared on shows like Famously Single, and Celebrity Fear Factor.

    Sammi went on to launch a clothing and accessories line, Sweetheart Styles.

    She also hosted a podcast called Just Sayin’ with Clare Galterio.

    Jersey Shore fans want Sammi back full time

    This season, Jersey Shore: Family Vacation has surprised everyone with the return of Sammi Giancola.

    Fans want Sammi to be featured on a permanent basis, and to replace Angelina Pivarnick.

    “[In] with Sammi. Out with Angelina,” wrote a fan on Twitter.

    “You can see that her playing the victim is actually orchestrated by her,” wrote someone else.

    More on Ronnie’s break and comeback

    The star’s lawyers also commented: “He is currently seeking medical treatment for psychological issues that he has ignored for a long time.

    “His only present goal is to address these issues professionally and make himself a better person and the best father he can be to his loving daughter.”

    However, on April 18, 2022, a source told The U.S. Sun that Ronnie has “quietly been filming since late January or early February of this year.”

    “The cast members initially weren’t sure he would be returning at all for this season.”

    On August 10, 2022, JSFV’s official Instagram unveiled a preview that showed Ronnie back on the program.
