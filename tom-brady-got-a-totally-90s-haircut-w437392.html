    Cue JTT. Tom Brady debuted a fresh new haircut on Tuesday, August 30, and the internet had a field day comparing him to everyone from Jonathan Taylor Thomas to Megyn Kelly.

    The 39-year-old Patriots quarterback — who doesn’t seem to have consulted his stylist wife, Gisele Bündchen — styled his volumized hair with a middle part, revealing a slight buzz above his ears, during a press conference.

    “Holy crap! Just realized that Tom #Brady was in ‘Home Improvement!’#Hair,” one observer wrote on Twitter, while another added: "And then I told my barber: Give me the Megyn Kelly."
