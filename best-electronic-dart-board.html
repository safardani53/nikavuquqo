    Best Electronic Dart Board !! Are you looking for a good electronic dart board to entertain your guests at home parties in 2023?

    There will be many who will argue that the traditional one is better than the newer electronic one. But we are here to give you reasons to love electronic dart boards.

    The traditional dart board is great for bars and pubs. However, if you have to get one for your home, it is practical to have electronic dart boards. They are attractive, have lights, quality darts and holes, and multiplayer options.

    One of the benefits of having an electronic one is that it tallies the score. Similarly, you are less likely to damage your walls and safer than the traditional boards.

    These are a few of the benefits of electronic boards over traditional ones. Here, we present nine of this year’s best electronic dart boards to prove our point further.

    We have prepared our list taking BarGames101 and AboveHouse as our primary references.

    9. Fat Cat 727 Electronic Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 18 x 17.2 x 1.9 inches
    • Weight: 2.8 Pounds

    For number 9, we have Fat Cat 727 Electronic Dart Board. It is an ideal dart board with a 13-inch target face that can easily fit in the cramped walls.

    It features 43 dart games with 200 scoring options and variations. Fat Cat 727 has a stylish look with classic red, green, and cream in a dark black color. It is built with durable thermal resin segments that can withstand the toughest throws.

    The target face of the dartboard is surrounded by a large missed dart catch ring that protects your wall from errant throws. Furthermore, the electronic board is engineered with concave segment holes to lock the shots and eliminate bounce-outs.

    Perfect for multiplayer, up to eight players can play simultaneously, allowing team play. The board comes with six soft-tip darts. It requires three AA batteries which are not included.

    8. WIN.MAX Electronic LED Electric Digital Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 24 x 20 x 2.8 inches
    • Weight: 4.92 Kilograms

    On number 8, we have the WIN.MAX Electronic LED Electric Digital. It is a durable and sturdy board that is easy to put up and gets the best dart-playing experience.

    The dart board has a sturdy ABS cabinet and built-in slots in the doors that can store four sets of darts and other dart accessories. It has a large LED display that records the score and fair play.

    Similarly, the dartboard features concave segment holes and ultra-thin wire that helps to prevent bounce-outs. It has a large catch ring surrounding the target face, creating a 360-degree landing zone.

    As a result, your walls are protected from any missed shots. Aside from these, it features 27 games with 195 scoring options, and up to eight players can play simultaneously.

    The WIN.MAX Electronic LED Electric Digital comes with 12 soft-tip starter darts, 40 soft-tip dart points, an AC power adapter, and a game manual.

    7. Viper Ion Electronic Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 22 x 19.5 x 1.2 inches
    • Weight: 4 Pounds

    The Viper Ion Electronic Dart Board is a new, top-quality dart board that has brought a new dimension to the dart games. 

    It features top-quality segments with a regulation-sized 15.5-inch dart board target area. Similarly, it also features concave segment holes engineered with a precision that locks in the shots.

    The board has a bright display and can be easily read from a distance. Its enhanced built-in LED lights and scoring provide a unique gameplay experience.

    It has 48 games with 315 variations, including 17 exclusive light-based games and optional moving target games. Furthermore, the Viper Ion features a motion-based, helicopter-spinning game.

    The dart board game supports up to eight players with various team combinations. It has six starter darts, mounting hardware, and a manual.

    6. Fat Cat Electronx Electronic Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 24 x 18.5 x 2.6 inches
    • Weight: 10.75 Pounds

    Our sixth-best electronic dart board is the Fat Cat Electronx. It is a dart board and cabinet hybrid that give hours of unlimited fun in the comfort of your home.

    The board is intelligently designed with durable thermoplastic segments that withstand tough throws. It has a compact 13.5-inch target area with precision-engineered concave segment holes.

    The large catch ring around the target’s face catches any missed shots, thus, protecting your walls. Similarly, the ultra-thin spider makes scoring easy and tracks all throws.

    Fat Cat Electronx features 34 games that up to eight players can play. It includes popular dart games with over 180 scoring options.

    Furthermore, the built-in cabinet and storage are equipped with dart holsters to store the darts and accessories away. The board has six starter soft-tip darts, mounting hardware, six spare tips, and an AC adapter.

    5. Bullshooter E-Bristle 1000 LED Electronic Dart Board Cabinet Set

    • Weight: 15 Pounds

    The next electronic dart board is Arachnid’s Bullshooter E-Bristle 1000 LED Electronic Dart Board Cabinet Set. It is one of the best electronic dart boards that provides players the playability of a bristle dartboard with the convenience of electronic scoring.

    The Bullshooter is durable and sturdy that is mounted in a contemporary-style cabinet. The cabinet has built-in dart holders, allowing you to easily tuck away your darts and accessories after playing.

    The board has a 13.5 inches target area with a large active score window with bright LEDs for maximum visibility. There are 38 different games and 183 variations you can choose from.

    Up to eight players can play simultaneously, but the Bullshooter also includes solo play, where you can play against the computer. Similarly, a unique interactive Heckler option applauds good throws and taunts players for bad throws.

    This board by Arachnid is ideal if you are looking for something unique. It comes with six steel tip darts, six soft tip darts, extra tips, and an AC adapter.

    4. Arachnid Cricket Maxx 1.0 Electronic Dart Board Cabinet Set

    • L x W x H: 27 x 22.5 x 5.5 inches
    • Weight: 14.3 Pounds

    For number 4, we have another electronic dart board by Arachnid. It is the Arachnid Cricket Maxx 1.0 Electronic Dart Board Cabinet Set.

    The dart board has a 13.5 inches target area. It includes 34 games with 183 variations, solo play, and heckler features. The heckler feature adds to the fun during the gameplay as it applauds good throws and mocks bad throws.

    The most notable feature of the dartboard is its large door-mounted X/O cricket display in the popular pub-style format. Similarly, the cabinet of the electronic dart board is wooden with a walnut finish which gives it a stylish look.

    Arachnid Cricket Maxx 1.0 comes with an E-Bristle target Steel & Soft Tip Darts. This dartboard is an ideal fit for your home and game room.

    3. Viper Neptune Electronic Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 21.5 x 26.5 x 3.5 inches
    • Weight: 20.3 Pounds

    We have the Viper Neptune Electronic Dart Board as number 3 for the best electronic dart board. It is a complete soft tip dart board setup that is easy to set up.

    The dart board has a regulation-sized 15.5 inches target area and top-quality segments. Similarly, the large catch ring around the target’s face catches missed shots and protects your walls.

    Furthermore, the ultra-thin spider allows for tighter shot groupings and a large surface area. It increases the ratio of landed shots while reducing bounce-outs.

    The unique feature is that the Viper Neptune allows for multiplayer with up to 16 players. It is the most among all the electronic dart boards featured on our list.

    Likewise, it features 57 games with over 300 scoring options. The dart board comes with six starters soft-tip darts, and an AC adapter.

    2. WIN.MAX Electronic Dart Board

    • L x W x H: 20 x 17 x 1 inches
    • Weight: 3.4 Pounds

    Our second-best pick is the WIN.MAX Electronic Dart Board. It is rated as one of the top dart boards on several internet sites.

    The dart board features a 13.5-inch target face with a crystal-clear LCD display. As a result, it helps to keep track of the scores efficiently and conveniently. It also features an ultra-thin net that reduces bounce-outs.

    Similarly, the net helps to increase your chance of throwing your best shots. The wide catch ring protects your walls from any errant throws. The automatic scoring and voice announcer further add to the fun.

    WIN.MAX Electronic Dart Board allows up to eight players and has 21 games and 65 variations. There is also a solo player option to have fun alone with a computer opponent.

    Up to eight individuals can play in multiplayer, and up to four can play in a single-player game. The board comes with 12 darts and an AC adapter.

    1. Arachnid Cricket Pro 800

    • L x W x H: 22.83 x 30.31 x 3.15 inches
    • Weight: 5 Kilograms

    The number 1 on our list of best electronic dartboard is the Arachnid Cricket Pro 800. It features the target area’s classic red, black, and yellow trademarked tournament classic look.

    The Arachnid Cricket Pro 800 has a regulation size 15.5-inch target area for tournament-style play. It has micro-thin segment dividers, which help to reduce bounce-outs.
