    Reinhold Andreas Messner is an Italian mountaineer, explorer and author. He made his first ascent to Mount Everest alone and with Peter Habeler, the first man to climb Mount Everest without needing oxygen. He was the first climber to climb the fourteen peaks at an altitude of 8,000 meters. Messner was the first person to cross Antarctica and Greenland by sled or dog sled. He also traveled alone through the Gobi Desert. Messner has published over 80 books on his experiences as a mountaineer and explorer. In 2018, he was jointly awarded the Princess of Asturias award to Krzysztof Wielicki in the sports category.
