    Luton Town vs Arsenal preview

    Arsenal make the trip to Luton's Kenilworth Road with a skip in their step after last weekend's results.

    The Gunners edged out Wolves at the Emirates before seeing Manchester City drop points at home to a depleted Tottenham in a six-goal thriller.

    The results leave Mikel Arteta's side top of the league after 14 matches with 33 points - two clear of Liverpool and three clear of Pep Guardiola's side.

    Though not exactly bursting with goals, Arsenal boast the best defensive record in the league having shipped just 11 goals in the 2023-24 season.

    And that should be a significant advantage against Luton, who've registered just 13 league goals this campaign - only Sheffield United have scored fewer.

    The Hatters succumbed to a 3-1 loss at Brentford on Saturday, and have picked up five points from a possible 18 at Kenilworth Road since their promotion.
