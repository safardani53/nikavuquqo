    Close To Getting Married?

    Michael Teutul’s Bio: Age & Height

    Born on 26 November 1978, Michael Joseph Teutul grew up in Suffern, New York, alongside his siblings. He has two brothers Paul Teutul Jr, Daniel Teutul, and a sister named Cristin Teutul.

    Michael is the youngest son of the founder of Orange County Choppers, Paul Teutul Sr., and his first wife, Paula Teutul.

    At the mere age of 14, Michael started working at his family’s company, the Orange County Iron Works. When his father, Paul Sr. and brother Paul Jr. began Orange County Choppers, he joined them as an assistant general manager.

    He also became part of the show's spin-off, American Chopper: Senior vs. Junior.

    On rare occasions, Michael would also help build choppers, and eventually, he built his bike. When the family business became the basis for the series American Chopper, Michael played his role in the show to provide comic relief.

    The TV personality had a hand in promoting the company as he appeared on Late Show with David Letterman, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, and Late Night with Conan O’Brien.

    Michael’s Net Worth

    As per Celebrity Net Worth, Michael has an estimated net worth of $2 million, which shouldn’t be unusual for a rising entrepreneur.

    Coming from a family with a successful business line, he has proved his rise through his efforts.

    Teutul's passion for painting led him to open a Wolfgang Gallery at the Hudson Valley heart in 2010.

    The gallery provides local painters a platform to showcase their work. His gallery also brings him a lot of money as well. Adding to that, Michael is also marketing a line of pasta sauces named FarQueue Products, LLC.

    He has also been on TV series such as My Name is Earl and King of the Hill.
