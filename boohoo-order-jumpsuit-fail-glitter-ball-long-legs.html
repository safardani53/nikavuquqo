    IT'S one of the most important dates in the fashion calendar - so it's no wonder we all agonise over what to wear to the annual Christmas party for months in advance.

    And while glitter has proved to be a HUGE trend (yet again) this festive season, one dissatisfied Boohoo shopper got a bit more sparkle than she bargained for when a jumpsuit she ordered made her look like a "melted glitter ball".

    It was love at first scroll for Olivia Burroughs when she came across the brand's Metallic Sequin Wide Leg jumpsuit on their website.

    And better still, it was reduced from £30 to £25 in the Black Friday sale.

    After hastily adding it to her shopping basket, Olivia, from Nottinghamshire, purchased the romper for her friend's upcoming Christmas party.

    But rather than making her legs look endless like it did on the model, the item that arrived instead drowned Olivia in fabric.

    And Olivia was so put out by the item that she didn't even go to the Christmas party in the end.

    After all, we can't imagine she actually wanted to turn up looking like a "melted disco ball with extra long legs".

    She said: "I couldn't believe the difference to the photo online, it was a real expectation vs reality of online shopping moment.

    "I immediately kicked off and decided I wasn't going to the party because of how my outfit looked - and it was too late for me to order anything else."

    Needless to say, the jumpsuit went straight back in the post - but not before Olivia posed for a hilarious selfie in her living room mirror.

    On the plus side, at least it's not as bad as this woman who claimed PrettyLittleThing "ruined" her New Years Eve by sending her the wrong dress.

    Moral of the story? ALWAYS buy important outfits with a day or two to spare.
